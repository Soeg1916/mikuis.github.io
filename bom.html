--- ### The Tale of Miku and Soeg Once upon a time, in a quaint village nestled between rolling hills and serene rivers, lived a boy named Soeg and a girl named Miku. They were childhood friends, inseparable and always dreaming of a future together. However, fate had different plans. When they were teenagers, Miku's family had to move to a distant city. The separation was heart-wrenching, but Soeg promised Miku that he would wait for her, no matter how long it took. Miku, with tears in her eyes, promised to return one day. Years passed, and Soeg remained true to his word. He wrote letters to Miku, pouring his heart into each one, but never received a reply. Despite the silence, he never lost hope. He believed that one day, Miku would come back to him. Miku, on the other hand, faced many challenges in the city. Her family struggled, and she had to work hard to support them. She missed Soeg dearly but couldn't find a way to reach out to him. She kept his memory alive in her heart, hoping that one day they would reunite. After ten long years, Miku finally returned to the village. She was nervous, unsure if Soeg would still be waiting for her. As she walked through the familiar streets, memories flooded back. She reached the old oak tree where they used to play and found Soeg sitting there, just as he had promised. Their eyes met, and in that moment, all the years of separation melted away. They embraced, tears of joy streaming down their faces. Soeg had waited for Miku, and she had finally come back to him. They shared their stories of struggle and perseverance, realizing that their love had only grown stronger over the years. Soon after, they got married in a beautiful ceremony under the old oak tree, surrounded by friends and family. Their love story became a legend in the village, a testament to the power of true love and unwavering faith. Miku and Soeg lived happily ever after, cherishing each moment together and never taking their love for granted. ---
